**Sep 2025 – Jan 2026**

Worked as part of the Eco-Drive engineering team on the development of two electric vehicles, **KAFO (Urban Concept)** and **QARA (Prototype)**, for participation in the **Shell Eco-marathon Qatar**. My responsibilities focused on **electrical systems**, **motor control**, **energy efficiency**, and **system integration** during both the pre-marathon and competition phases.

### Key Contributions
- Designed and implemented the complete **electrical wiring architecture**, ensuring safety, reliability, and compliance with Shell Eco-marathon technical inspection requirements.
- Programmed and tuned the **motor controller** to achieve smooth acceleration, stable operation, and optimal energy efficiency.
- Performed **system-level testing** and iterative tuning to optimize powertrain efficiency and vehicle performance under real driving conditions.
- Contributed to **energy management optimization** by analyzing motor controller behavior and minimizing electrical losses.
- Supported electrical integration of **batteries, controllers, protection devices, and control interfaces**.

### Competition Achievements
- Successfully passed **Technical Inspection** for both vehicles under strict safety and engineering standards.
- **KAFO** competed among **9 vehicles** in the Urban Concept category.
- **QARA Prototype** completed **four consecutive laps** in its first competition appearance.
