This project studies household energy consumption and designs a tailored solar photovoltaic (PV) system with appropriate battery storage.

Goals included precise load analysis, selecting inverters and charge controllers, designing the system topology, and evaluating financial and technical feasibility. The final output is a comprehensive report detailing calculations, diagrams, and analysis for efficient and reliable energy management.
